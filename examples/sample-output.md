## J. CapCut Editing Checklist

- [ ] New project, ratio 9:16 (1080×1920).

- [ ] Background: black solid.

### 2. Opening Frame (0–4s)

- [ ] Subtitle pertama (S01–S02): "He slipped on / a question nobody asked."

### 3. Subtitle Style
- [ ] Font: Bebas Neue atau Anton (bold sans-serif).
