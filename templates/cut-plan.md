# Cut Plan Template

Tabel ini mendefinisikan urutan final dari short-form content.

## Labels

| Label | Arti | Action |
|-------|------|--------|
| **KEEP** | Segment dipertahankan | Potong dari source, masukkan ke timeline |
| **CUT** | Segment dihapus | Tidak masuk final edit |
| **GAP** | Insert point | Tambahkan transisi, B-roll, text overlay, atau pause |

## Template

```markdown
| # | Start | End | Duration | Label | Audio Source | Alasan |
|---|-------|-----|----------|-------|-------------|--------|
| 1 | HH:MM:SS | HH:MM:SS | Xs | KEEP | Original / VO / Mix | [alasan singkat] |
| 2 | — | — | Xs | GAP | — | [jenis insert: transition/B-roll/text] |
| 3 | HH:MM:SS | HH:MM:SS | Xs | KEEP | Original / VO | [alasan singkat] |
```

## Audio Source Options

| Source | Kapan Digunakan |
|--------|----------------|
| **Original** | Dialog asli cukup engaging, pace bagus, audio quality OK |
| **VO** | Perlu kondensasi, reframing, atau pace adjustment |
| **Mix** | VO narration di atas original audio (volume dikecilkan) |

## Duration Guidelines

| Platform | Optimal | Maximum | Hook Window |
|----------|---------|---------|-------------|
| TikTok | 30-45s | 60s | 1-3s |
| YouTube Shorts | 40-55s | 60s | 3s |
| Reels | 30-45s | 90s | 3s |

## Checklist Sebelum Finalisasi

- [ ] Total KEEP duration dalam range target platform
- [ ] Tidak ada 2 GAP berturut-turut (selalu ada KEEP di antara)
- [ ] Hook segment di posisi #1
- [ ] CTA atau payoff di posisi terakhir
- [ ] Setiap KEEP punya audio source yang jelas
